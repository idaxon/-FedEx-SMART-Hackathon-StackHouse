About Us

At StackHouse, we’re redefining logistics by making warehouse booking seamless and efficient. Whether you need short-term storage or long-term solutions, we’ve got you covered across the country.

Founded by Daksh Mishra, a second-year student from BML Munjal University, StackHouse was born out of a simple idea: to make logistics as easy as ordering pizza (but without pineapple debates). With innovation and a touch of humor, we’re here to simplify your logistics journey.

Join us in revolutionizing the way businesses store and move their goods—because logistics should be smart, simple, and maybe even fun!
